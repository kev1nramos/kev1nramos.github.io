Why Blog?

The idea is simple, learning and writing about learning, whether it is about new topics or previously covered.

The origin of the blog is born in the year of the thesis, where in my case, there is the practical part and the theoretical part.

A fun fact inherent to the human being is procrastination and the blog came to help in this sense.

The themes addressed in the blog will be vast, I like to learn and I don't want my blog to be a place where it will impose barriers at all.

In the future, I will update the blog's code structure, because its current structure is not scalable.

Each and every piece of code may be used by third parties. If you want to use it as a personal page and need help, I will be happy to explain the structure.

Any improvement or suggestion of themes to study, send via email. I will analyze and answer as soon as possible.
