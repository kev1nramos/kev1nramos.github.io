Why did I change the theme after 7 months?

First of all, if I am going to write about all the stages of my thesis, it would not make sense to pass on such an important one, the change of theme!

As it is in the title, why did I change the theme after 7 months?

The first theme focused on Code Generation through Natural Language, the complexity was enormous for me to create or improve something that I thought would have an scientific impact, in less than 1 year.

I'll probably talk more about this topic after I finish my thesis, however, I leave here a research center that you should follow if you're really interested in this topic:

  • Language Technologies Institute - CMU

The current theme is Attribute Extraction from Product Description and the reason behind this choice touches on two points:

  1. The exponential increase of e-Commerce

  2. I was finishing up my online store and wanted to maximize my time.

But what is Attribute Extraction from Product Description?

To create an online store, among several things, it is necessary to have a database and most of the creators have only the title, description and images of the products.

These same filters come from the attributes of the products and my proposal is to automatically extract the attributes through the title and description of the products.

According to Baymard, if the "Filtering" experiences was well implemented and filtering with availability and logic, the results can be 350% and 650% over the performance average score of the remaining 49 top US e-commerce websites, it's shows how important products attributes are. (Source: Baymard)
