QuestChain

QuestChain is a web and mobile app platform that, through gamification and blockchain, intends to help refugees finding a job.

Project Overview

An app was created in the context of the hackathon promoted by the Calouste Gulbenkian Foundation in 2018.

The platform consisted of an app where the gamification concept was the main feature.

The implementation was a support platform for refugees at source, i.e. linking the refugees in the camps to the companies in order to facilitate their integration into the country.

To do so, it would be necessary to check the qualifications and since there is no way through the normal channels (diplomas, certificates, etc...) we consider the need to carry out a practical test specific to the area that the refugee says he or she is aware of.

Businesses play an important role in the integration of refugees and internships would be a gateway.

To ensure that both the internship and the qualifications/qualifications were assessed correctly and coherently, a 360-degree assessment was carried out, in which there was a responsible external person who assessed the two entities, along with the refugee's assessment of the company and the company to the refugee. In this way, the bias would be reduced.

The platform also included partnerships with companies, foundations and non-governmental entities and were important for the success of this project. (At the time, we already had partnerships with 1 foundation and 1 company and 2 NGOs but for reasons of confidentiality, I will not disclose the identity).

The platform had more features however, will not be described due to the possibility of reopening the project.
