QuestChain

QuestChain is a web and mobile app platform that, through gamification and blockchain, intends to help refugees finding a job.

Project Overview

The app was created in the context of the hackathon promoted by KPMG and Outsystems as part of the ISCTE-IUL IT fair, called FISTA, in 2017.

The platform consisted of an app with QR Codes and Beacons, with the gamification concept as main feature. The goal was to make partnerships with large companies in exchange for discounts. The value for companies was to increase the value of the brand through the perception that consumers would stay through their attitude of social responsibility. Normally, a brand associated with social responsibility brings more notoriety and increases profits.

Because the application was developed in Outsystems, there is a necessity to access the platform so that the developed environment is not removed, which subsequently happened. As such, there is no sample of the prototype and the value of the project is only by the idea.
