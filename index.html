Who Am I

22 years old, many books, one degree and one company.

I'm a Computer Science and Business Management masters student, code generation through natural language is my thesis. Running a jewelry ecommerce business called Charme 24 is my daily routine.

BSc - Computer Science and Business Management

The Computer Science and Business Management graduates are prepared to develop activities related with information systems management, teaching and research, or even to develop their own innovative products and services through the launch of new startup companies.

MSc - Computer Science and Business Management

My thesis focuses on two fields of research: natural language and code generation. The goal is to generate code through natural language, which would allow anyone to put their idea into practice in their native language, without having to learn new concepts.

IT Director - ISCTE Junior Consulting

ISCTE Junior Consulting is a business consulting organisation ran by undergraduate and postgraduate students from ISCTE-IUL.

Co-Founder - Charme 24

Charme 24 is a jewelry ecommerce website based on Ourivesaria Charme, a physical company with more then two decades. Maximize the experience and satisfaction of our clients is our main goal!
